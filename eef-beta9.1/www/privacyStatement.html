Cookies

This site places small files, known as 'cookies', onto your computer to collect information about how you browse. These cookies aren't used to identify you personally.

Some cookies are necessary for using this site, including a cookie that remembers your cookie preferences from previous visits. These can only be disabled within your browser settings

Other cookies are used for understanding how you use the website. You can opt-in and opt-out of this at any time by clicking the cookie button in the bottom-right of the screen.

You will normally see a message on the site before we store a cookie on your computer.

Find out more about how to manage cookies.

Measuring site usage

We use Google Analytics software to collect information about how you use this website. We do this to help make sure the site is meeting your needs and to help us make improvements.

We store information about:

  • the pages you visit and how long you spend on each page
  • how you got to scotland.shinyapps.io
  • what you click on while you're visiting the scotland.shinyapps.io Web Apps

Any information we get is received in a way that we can't identify anyone by it. For example, we never receive your name or address.

We don't make any attempt to find out the identities of people visiting the site, and we don't allow Google to do this either.

All information we get through cookies and analytics will be treated in confidence. We will never sell, trade or give your details to third parties, unless we're required to do it by law.

Find out more about security and privacy in Google Analytics.
